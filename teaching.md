## Teaching and Mentorship Impact

## Instructional contributions
- Development and instruction of an advanced **Security Analytics** course integrating theory, data-driven methods, and real-world security case studies (phishing, deception, adversarial behavior).
- Research-integrated teaching at undergraduate and graduate levels (projects aligned with contemporary cybersecurity analytics problems).

## Mentorship outcomes
- Mentorship of undergraduate researchers, MS/PhD students, and collaborators leading to peer-reviewed publications and professional development.
- Student placements and career outcomes include high-impact organizations such as **MITRE**, the **National Security Agency (NSA)**, and the **Department of Homeland Security (DHS)**, along with industry and academia.

## Recognition
- **University of Houston Lifetime Faculty Award for Mentoring Undergraduate Research** (career achievement).
